How to ...

Follow the link to go to the webtimer. You'll see the current count down on the right side of the screen. Here you can also add comments and suggestions. All the collected comments (of you and the rest of the audience) are shown here as well.

On the left you'll see the timer for the next interval (top) and the next next interval (). You can alter these intervals by adding or subtracting 10 seconds.
